PROJECT IDEA

Overview

The project is a mobile app to design furniture home by using augmented reality. As we know, taking home plenty of gadgets patterns to try out all the collaboration in the owner area is problematic. This application allows you to put the environment of your room into the phone’s screen, then you can change the background wall color, move the bed’s position, add or remove the table and chair, etc without delivering those directly to your house.

PROJECT IDEA

Motivation

In the current industry 4.0, all professional companies want to reach the highest needs of customers. One of the technological peaks that are gradually becoming a trend of social development is augmented reality (AR). As everyone knows, customizing your own room in various ideas with reality technologies is more actual, more saving, safer and faster, that help customers and sellers avoid a bunch of problems such as time-wasting, fee shipping, health, etc. This application is a quality project for a start-up mobile company because it can earn money by collaborating with furniture shops, or restore or customize factories.

PROJECT IDEA

Description

Firstly, this app requires a smartphone or a tablet that has ARKit or ARCore assistant, so this can connect your phone to the practical environment with AR technology. Then, we will need to turn on the camera, scanning around your space slowly and moving 360 degrees, to create a 3D environment, after that the system will start to analyze the data of that scanned area. Once the system has completed analyzing the data, the room space detail will be saved into application memory, this helps you not need to do step one again. You can open resources and pick which spaces you want to try furniture on.

Secondly, connecting your chosen space to the furniture companies that you are going to buy, picking a style you love, they will provide you dozens of trials that you can play around with wall painting colors, textures, cabinets, tools, etc in a 3D environment. Moreover, this app will recommend which materials are suitable for your kind of ground, wall, and space based on the analyzed data. Also, they will show popular ideas from trending rooms for you to compare. All these functions ensure your room design will be furnished fully and appointed perfectly.

PROJECT IDEA

Tools and technologies

As I research, some design programs are necessary for this project such as AutoCAD, 3ds Studio Max, and REVIT to create 3D gadgets based on the requirements of the company. VR technology is also a compulsory tool that can combine the real and virtual world and real-time interaction, which helps this room design app succeed almost completely. The more grown edition of your phone’s camera, the more convenient it is for you to move, drag, remove, expand and shrink everything smoothly without bug error, for example, ARKit tool of iPhone from iPhone 8 with iOS 11 and above.

PROJECT IDEA

Skill Required

As an IT developer, this project requires me to be professional at some coding languages such as Swift (iOS), JAVA (Android), and good at a cross-platform framework like JavaScript (React Native) or Dart (Flutter) to bring good experiments for users. I also need to learn more about reality technologies and about design programs which is also necessary to understand how everything launches.

PROJECT IDEA

Outcome

People can try to customize the room in their own way without asking or calling anyone if this project is successful. People will save more time, money, and health. Couples, families can show their own ideas just by watching on the phone’s screen, sharing and giving opinions without conflicting or putting much effort. This development helps not only mobile companies but also start-up furniture stores approach customers easier and quicker than the pass, making delivery and shopping become more comfortable and appropriate.

I will discuss about Virtual Reality
What does it do?

Virtual Reality (English: Virtual Reality - VR) is a mock-up experience that can be the same or fully dissimilar from the actual world. In addition to creating virtual spaces, VR technology can actually interact with users through gestures and many different senses such as hearing, smell, and touch. At this moment, it serves many areas, such as entertainment, education, medical, and even military. For example, the application of virtual reality in education is a breakthrough in teaching methods, new approaches for learners and teachers, instead of learning theory, it will move to practical learning, practical experience through 3D simulation, LAB rooms, so the lessons and knowledge will be realistic, detailed, quick to understand, easy to remember, attracting learners, much higher efficiency. times compared to the old way. On the other hand, VR would bring strongly interact with human feelings, if watching a simulated movie with VR glasses, users can move around the movie space, can turn their head to the left, to the right to change the viewing angle. As for a regular movie, that is completely impossible. Moreover, some special VR virtual reality systems will integrate many more factors such as wind, water, light effects, vibration, etc to increase user’s emotions when using VR. In the next 3 years, I believe the next social media is a presence of VR world. Imagine where a brand could invite their brand ambassadors to try a product without getting on a plane? A place where a brand can convey press releases but not with pre-written ones but actually be there to share the news with the community of journalists and meet each other in social VR. There are many opportunities for brands and content creators.
